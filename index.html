Skin Concerns

Acne & Blemishes

  • Mild Acne: occasional pimples, whiteheads, blackheads
  • Severe Acne: cystic or inflamed acne
  • Oily / Combination Skin: prone to clogged pores

Dryness

  • Flaky or rough texture
  • Tightness after cleansing
  • Prone to irritation

Aging

  • Fine lines & wrinkles
  • Loss of elasticity
  • Uneven skin tone

Pigmentation

  • Dark spots
  • Uneven skin tone
  • Sun damage

Sensitivity

  • Redness or irritation
  • Burning sensation
  • Reacts to products

Enlarged Pores

  • Visible pores
  • Oily shine
  • Blackheads
